Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tsp garlic minced
  • 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups parmesan cheese freshly grated

Steps

  1. Add butter and cream to large skillet
  2. Simmer over low heat for 2 minutes
  3. Whisk in garlic, italian seasoning, salt, pepper for 1 minute
  4. Whisk in parmesan until melted
  5. Serve immediately to avoid coagulation
